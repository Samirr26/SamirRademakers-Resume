Biography

My name is Samir Rademakers, I am 19 years old and I am from The Netherlands. When I was 16 years old I moved out to study technical computer science in Breda, a city in The Netherlands about an hour away from my hometown. Before I went to university I I graduated high school with bilingual education, therefore my English is almost as good as my Dutch.

From a very young age I have always been interested in computers and in the last two years of high school I also chose to take computer science as an extra subject. After that I started studying technical computer science at Avans Hogeschool in Breda. Ever since I started studying computer science, I discovered that I have a true passion for developing software.

In my free time I like to play games, hang out with friends, go out, do fitness and travel. Also in my free time I like to work on my own computer science related projects. One of those projects for example is this website.
